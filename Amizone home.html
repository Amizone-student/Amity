UPGRADATION OF PROGRAMME OF STUDENTS FROM THREE YEARS HONOURS/REGULAR UG PROGRAMMES TO 4 YEARS HONs
  • 29 May 2024
  • Dear Student, 1. This is to apprise you that NEP 2020 stressed on UG programmes of 4 years duration. Also, UGC in their recent notifications have reiterated the provision given in the Curriculum and Credit Framework which states that, “All existing students who are enrolled and pursuing UG programme as per Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) are eligible to pursue 4 years undergraduate programme”. 2. AUUP, accordingly has made the provision for enabling all students undergoing 3-years Honours/Regular UG Programmes of B.A./B.Sc./BCA/B.Com. of 2023-26, 2022-2025 and 2021-24 batches for upgradation to 4-years UG Programmes (Honours/Honours with Research). 3. The 4-years UG Programmes (Honours/Honours with Research) will have following advantages over the 3 years Honours/Regular Programmes: a) Students completing 4-years UG programmes (Honours/Honours with Research) with 7.5 CGPA, can directly appear for UGC NET examination and enroll for the Ph.D Degree Programmes. b) Students completing 4-years Honours/Honours with research UG Programmes, can pursue a one-year Master’s Programme. c) Students can apply directly for one year Masters’ Programme in Foreign Universities. 4. The extra credits earned by students during 3-years honours/regular Programmes will be either adjusted with the same core courses/ elective courses of same discipline if offered in 4-years UG Programmes (Honours/ Honours with Research) or as 15% extra credits earned as per existing guidelines. 5. The students of 3 years Honours/Regular Programmes must take a note of the above advantages to avail this opportunity to upgrade their Programme. The students can apply for programme upgradation prior to commencement of Semester (Odd/ Even). 6. The students of Final years of Class of 2024 are specially permitted to avail this opportunity of programme upgradation. 7. There are no charges for upgradation of Programmes from 3-years Honours/Regular to 4-years UG Programmes (Honours/Honours with Research). 8. The Last date for application for programme upgradation is 30th June 2024. 9. For any further clarifications students can approach to their Programme Leaders (PLs)/ HoIs for any guidance for upgradation of Programme from 3-years Honours/Regular to 4-years UG Programmes (Honours/Honours with Research). Thanks & Regards, Prof. (Dr) Raj Kamal Kapur Officiating Registrar

    NEP SAARTHI CLUB
  • 19 Mar 2024
  • Office of Dean Student Support & Academic Affairs NOTICE FOR STUDENTS NEP SAARTHI CLUB Date – 19 March 2024 Dear Students, This is to inform all that the NEP SAARTHI (National Education Policy - Student Ambassadors for Academic Reforms in Transforming Higher Education in India) Club is being introduced as a pioneering initiative at Amity University Uttar Pradesh, Noida Campus. Working with the National Education Policy 2020 as our guidelines, which aims to reform education in India by boosting holistic growth, and ensure accessible quality education for all students, NEP SAARTHI (National Education Policy - Student Ambassadors for Academic Reforms in Transforming Higher Education in India) is an ambassador for NEP 2020, responsible for raising awareness with relevant information, and collecting student feedback through events, social media campaigns, and NEP help desks, organised by our club. The NEP SAARTHI Club aims to educate students about the transformative potential of NEP 2020 in revolutionizing higher education. Membership is open to Amity University Uttar Pradesh, Noida Campus students from all disciplines. Membership in the NEP SAARTHI Club offers numerous opportunities: • Acquire valuable insights into educational reforms and actively contribute to shaping the educational landscape. • Participate in engaging activities such as quizzes, debates, and collaborative projects designed to facilitate a deeper understanding of NEP 2020. • Network with fellow students, faculty members, and industry professionals dedicated to educational reform. • Benefit from the expertise of guest speakers, experts, and policymakers, enhancing your understanding of NEP 2020 implementation. • Make a meaningful impact within our university community and beyond, influencing the direction of educational policy in India. • Develop essential leadership skills, foster personal growth, and cultivate valuable connections for future endeavors. Following club coordinator positions are open for application: • Vice President (Male) • Member Secretary • Treasurer To join the club, please select NEP SAARTHI CLUB on the Amizone Portal, under the university club section and also fill the membership form using the link provided below - https://forms.gle/PqcBC2kGoTTM5AGGA For any queries, contact – Mr. Amrendra Pratap Singh - +91 9044086067 Ms Ishikaa - +91 6283027477 Ms. Sarisha - +91 9830079084 Best Wishes, Prof. (Dr.) Alpana Kakkar Dean Student Support and Academic Affairs

    President Message

    Dear Students,

    Over the last many weeks we have sadly come to know of some of our students losing a parent or both to Covid-19 complications. Though we have been doing all we can to give them emotional support, we have now decided to go a step further.

    If any current student at Amity University UP loses a parent that was supporting their fees for education and is facing financial difficulties because of that, we will sponsor the remaining full academic fees till they complete their current degree at Amity University UP.

    If you know of any of your fellow students who needs this help, please inform the Dean Student Welfare at dsw@amity.edu who will assess each individual case.

    In these times of stress and anxiety, it is also very important to take care of your mental health. As you know, our professional Amity Counsellors and Psychologists are available 24 hours a day for you. Do use this service any time you need by calling 18001026489.

    Wishing you good health.

    All the best.

    Atul Chauhan

    ACADEMIC BANK OF CREDIT (ABC) ID

    All enrolled students are required to register in the Academic Bank of Credits (ABC). ABC is a new revolutionary system which offers several benefits to the students such as flexibility and mobility, lifelong learning, customized learning paths, enhanced career opportunities etc. It will store the credits earned by the students on successful completion of the course of study. These credits can be transferred across educational institutions and will obviate the requirement of physically carrying the marksheets and other necessary documents. ABC has been declared mandatory by UGC for all enrolled students.

    Therefore, it is mandatory for each students to create their Academic Bank of Credit-ID (ABC-ID) on NAD as per provision of the UGC for uploading of data of students on Digi Locker (NAD) through link: https://digilocker.meripehchaan.gov.in/signup or visit : www.abc.gov.in. 

    Procedure to Create ABC ID:

    1. Visit www.abc.gov.in
    2. Click on my account >student
    3. For new user-click on "Sign up for Meri Pehchaan".
    4. Enter mobile number; you will get OTP on registered mobile number.
    5. Fill all necessary details and click on verify.
    6. Student will get ABC-ID.
